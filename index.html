Customised Visual Schedules — designed for every child

Every child is different. We create bespoke visual schedules tailored to your child's age, communication level, language, and daily routine — so it actually works for them.

"No two children learn, communicate, or experience the world the same way."

Our ready-made packs are a great starting point — but sometimes your child needs something more specific. A schedule for their exact morning routine. Cards in their home language. Images that reflect their world. A layout that matches how their therapist or school works.

That is what a customised schedule is. Built around your child, not a template.

What we can create for you
01
Any age group
From toddlers learning their first routine to teenagers managing complex school days. We design cards that match developmental stage — simple bold images for young children, detailed step-by-step cards for older ones.
02
Any language
Hindi, English, or both. Tamil, Marathi, Gujarati, Bengali. We can label your cards in your family's home language so your child connects with them immediately — because words should feel familiar, not foreign.
03
Any routine
Morning, school, therapy sessions, mealtime, bedtime, travel, medical visits, transitions between activities. If it matters to your child's day, we can build a schedule around it.
We understand every child is different

Built around your child, not a template

Children with autism, ADHD, sensory processing differences, communication delays, or any other neurodiverse profile often thrive with structure — but only when that structure makes sense to them. A schedule with the wrong images, the wrong words, or the wrong sequence can confuse more than it helps.

When you request a customised schedule, we start by understanding your child — their current routine, what they respond to, what causes friction, and what language they use at home. Then we design a schedule that fits their life, not a generic one they have to adapt to.

Language is not a barrier

India is home to hundreds of languages and dialects. Yet most visual schedule tools are in English only. We believe every parent should be able to show their child a schedule in the language they hear at home — whether that is Hindi, Tamil, Marathi, Gujarati, Kannada, Telugu, Bengali, or Punjabi.

We can create fully bilingual cards with both your home language and English, or single-language cards if that is what your child needs. The words should feel like yours.

Designed with therapists and educators in mind

Are you an occupational therapist, speech-language pathologist, special educator, or school coordinator? We work with professionals too. We can create consistent card sets for use across clinic and home, or design classroom schedules that align with your teaching approach. Clinic license packs are available for multiple-child use.

What to send us
  • Your child's age and communication level
  • Their daily routine — morning, school, therapy, bedtime
  • Language preference (Hindi / English / both / other)
  • Any specific activities or transitions they find difficult
  • Whether you need print-ready PDFs, digital cards, or both
Turnaround

We respond to every request within 48 hours. Custom schedules are typically ready within 5–7 working days. Urgent requests can be accommodated — just mention it in your message.

Pricing

Pricing is shared after we understand your requirements. Custom schedules start from ₹499. Clinic and school packs are priced separately. We never want cost to be a barrier — talk to us.
